<div class=“hymn”><h4 class=“hymn-num”>216</h4><h4 class=“hymn-title”>MORE ABOUT JESUS WOULD I KNOW</h4><ol id=“song”>   


<li>	More about Jesus would I know,
	More of His grace to others show;
	More of His saving fulness see,
	More of His love who died for me.

<span>	More, more about Jesus,
		More, more about Jesus:
		More of His saving fulness see,
		More of His love who died for me.	 
</span>

</li><li>	More about Jesus let me learn,
	More of His holy will discern;
	Spirit of God, my Teacher be,
	Showing the things of Christ to me.

</li><li>	More about Jesus, in His Word,
	Holding communion with my Lord;
	Hearing His voice in every line,
	Making each faithful saying mine.
<span>	More, more about Jesus,
		More, more about Jesus:
		More of His saving fulness see,
		More of His love who died for me.	 
</span>


</li><li>	More about Jesus, on His throne,
	Riches in glory all His own:
	More of His kingdom’s sure increase;
	More of His coming, Prince of Peace.


</li></ol></div>
